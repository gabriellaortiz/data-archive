How to download your data:

  1. Visit takeout.google.com

  2. Be sure you are logged into your personal google account, not a work or university account (any account you use most).

  3. Deselect all, scroll to "My Activity" and select it.

  4. Click "Multiple Formats" on the left.

  5. Click "HTML" and switch to "JSON."

  6. Click Next Step.

  7. Send download link via email, export once, create export.

  8. Wait for the link to come into your Google inbox.

  9. Once you recieve the .zip file, open and navigate to the "search" folder.

  10. Upload "MyActivity.json" file below.
